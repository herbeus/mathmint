Questions parents & teachers ask

What grade do kids learn mixed operations in?

Mixed practice usually starts in Grade 2 with a blend of addition and subtraction within 20, adds multiplication and division from Grade 3 once those facts are learned, and builds to a full four-operation review by Grade 6. Pick the grade above that matches your child.

Why practise mixed operations instead of one at a time?

A single-operation sheet tells a child which method to use before they even read the problem. A mixed sheet takes that hint away, so they have to decide whether to add, subtract, multiply or divide — the exact skill they need for word problems and tests. Master each operation on its own first, then mix them here.

How can my child practise mixed operations every day?

Five minutes a day beats one long session a week. Every click generates a fresh sheet with new problems and a matching answer key, so you'll never run out — print a new one each morning.
