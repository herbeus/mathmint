Questions parents & teachers ask

How do I help my child learn the 9 times table?

The finger trick: hold up ten fingers, fold the one you're multiplying by — tens on the left, ones on the right. The digits always add to 9. Print a fresh 9 times table worksheet, practise a little every day, and use the answer key to check — short, regular sessions beat one long cram.

What order are the facts in?

Each worksheet lists 9 × 1 through 9 × 12 in order, so kids can see the pattern build. Want them shuffled for a real recall test? Use the Mixed or Multiply mode in the builder instead.
