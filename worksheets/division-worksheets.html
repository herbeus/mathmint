Questions parents & teachers ask

What grade do kids learn division in?

Division starts in Grade 3 as the inverse of multiplication, with problems that always divide evenly into a whole number, moves to all the division facts to 12 in Grade 4, and reaches larger divisors up to 20 by Grade 6. Pick the grade above that matches your child.

Do these division worksheets have remainders?

No — every problem divides evenly into a whole number, so kids build confidence with clean answers first. Because division is the inverse of multiplication, a child who knows 6 × 7 = 42 can see that 42 ÷ 7 = 6. Remainders and long division come later.

How can my child practise division every day?

Five minutes a day beats one long session a week. Every click generates a fresh sheet with new problems and a matching answer key, so you'll never run out — print a new one each morning.

Are these division worksheets free?

Yes — every grade from Grade 3 to Grade 6 is free to generate and print, with answer keys, no account and no watermark.
