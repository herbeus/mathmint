Questions parents & teachers ask

How do I help my child learn the 4 times table?

Double, then double again. 4 × 7 → double 7 is 14, double that is 28. Print a fresh 4 times table worksheet, practise a little every day, and use the answer key to check — short, regular sessions beat one long cram.

What order are the facts in?

Each worksheet lists 4 × 1 through 4 × 12 in order, so kids can see the pattern build. Want them shuffled for a real recall test? Use the Mixed or Multiply mode in the builder instead.
