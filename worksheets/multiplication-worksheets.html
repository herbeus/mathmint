Questions parents & teachers ask

What grade do kids learn multiplication in?

Multiplication starts in Grade 2 with the first times tables up to 10, builds to the full tables to 12 in Grade 3 — the year fact fluency matters most — and stretches to larger factors up to 20 by Grade 6. Pick the grade above that matches your child.

Does my child need to know the times tables first?

Knowing the times tables to 12 is exactly what these worksheets build. The Grade 2–3 sheets drill the facts; by Grade 4 they should be automatic. If a single table is shaky, practise that one on its own first, then come back here to mix them all together.

How can my child practise multiplication every day?

Five minutes a day beats one long session a week. Every click generates a fresh sheet with new problems and a matching answer key, so you'll never run out — print a new one each morning.

Are these multiplication worksheets free?

Yes — every grade from Grade 2 to Grade 6 is free to generate and print, with answer keys, no account and no watermark.
