Questions parents & teachers ask

What is skip counting by 10s?

Skip counting by 10s means counting up in steps of 10 — 10, 20, 30, 40… instead of one at a time. Counting by 10s keeps the ones digit at 0 while the tens climb: 10, 20, 30… It's the foundation of place value and of counting dimes and dollars.

What grade is counting by 10s for?

Counting by 10s is usually taught in Kindergarten–Grade 2. These worksheets keep the sequences short and give a few numbers as anchors, so they suit early learners building number sense.
