Questions parents & teachers ask

How do I help my child learn the 6 times table?

Know your 5×? Then 6× is just 5× plus one more group: 6 × 7 = 35 + 7 = 42. Print a fresh 6 times table worksheet, practise a little every day, and use the answer key to check — short, regular sessions beat one long cram.

Each worksheet lists 6 × 1 through 6 × 12 in order, so kids can see the pattern build. Want them shuffled for a real recall test? Use the Mixed or Multiply mode in the builder instead.
