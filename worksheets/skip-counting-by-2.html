Questions parents & teachers ask

What is skip counting by 2s?

Skip counting by 2s means counting up in steps of 2 — 2, 4, 6, 8… instead of one at a time. Counting by 2s is a child's first taste of multiplication and the line between odd and even numbers. Every answer is an even number: 2, 4, 6, 8…

What grade is counting by 2s for?

Counting by 2s is usually taught in Kindergarten & Grade 1. These worksheets keep the sequences short and give a few numbers as anchors, so they suit early learners building number sense.
